The recipe based on this youtube video:
    Ingredients:
  • 200ml Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 100g Sugar
  • 50ml Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp Powder
  • A pinch of Salt
  • 250g Flour

First step

Beat the yolks with sugar, add milk and beat well again

Step two

When you have whipped the mixture with milk, gradually add the flour in 2-3 stages. Mix at the slowest speed. Then add baking powder and vegetable oil

Step three

Beat egg whites until stiff. Then add them in several steps to the main mixture. Stir slowly so that the mass does not fall off

Step four

Heat up the skillet well. Do not add oil, the pan must be dry! Pour the mass into the pan, if bubbles appear, the pancake can be turned over

Yummy!

Done, it turned out very tasty and fluffy pancakes! Pour them with syrup, honey or jam, enjoy!
